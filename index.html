About Infinity Pictures

Founded with a passion for capturing life's most precious moments, Infinity Pictures brings over a decade of professional photography experience to every project. Our team specializes in wedding photography, portrait sessions, and event coverage across Sri Lanka.

We believe that every moment tells a story, and our mission is to capture those stories with artistic vision and technical excellence. From intimate family portraits to grand wedding celebrations, we approach every project with the same dedication to quality and creativity.

Based in Kandy, our photography services have expanded to serve clients throughout Sri Lanka. We combine traditional photography techniques with modern technology to deliver stunning images that stand the test of time.
